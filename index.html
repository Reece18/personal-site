About Me

My name is Reece Roguski, I am 29 years old and before Novemeber 2017 I was a truck driver and house transporter in Bunbury. I Began self teaching myself front-end web development in Novemeber and fell inlove with the ability to create, design and the fantastic community surrounding the industry. I continued to study and attend social meet ups (a Huge thank you to the fenders community for being so welcoming) and was lucky enough to be offered a 3 month internship at Ninja Dojo software , and then to be hired as a junior front-end developer after the internship. Working at Ninja Dojo has helped expose me to multiple CMS's and coding languages. Although still a Junior and new to the industry my strongest skills lie in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery

With a basic understanding of:

  • PHP
  • Python
  • Mysql
  • WordPress Gutenberg
  • OctoberCMS
  • Github

I am incredibly self-motivated, hard working, trustworthy and determined to keep learning and filling in the gaps in my skillset with a passion for UI/UX.
